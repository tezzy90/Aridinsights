## 1. Core Product Thesis: The Artifact Is the Product
Arid Insights is an **artifact-producing system**, not a dashboard-first SaaS.
Institutional buyers do not primarily pay for visualizations. They pay for:
1.  **Defensible basis of reliance**
2.  **Reduced late-stage reversal risk**
3.  **Audit survivability**
4.  **Repeatability across assets and portfolios**

The platform’s UI, analytics, and AI synthesis are delivery and acceleration layers. The product, in the reliance sense, is the **Frozen Decision Artifact**.

## 2. Artifact Taxonomy
We produce four classes of artifacts with strict separation of authority.

### 2.1 Canonical Record Artifacts (Internal System-of-Record)
Normalized, evidence-linked building blocks.
*   **Utility Rate Record**: Verified schedules, effective dates.
*   **Permit Authority Map**: Who controls what, under which statutes.
*   **Constraint Record**: Moratoria, conservation requirements.

### 2.2 Frozen Decision Artifacts (Externally Relied-Upon)
Decision-grade outputs appended to underwriting/diligence files.
*   **Must be:** Immutable, Time-Indexed, Bounded, Traceable.
*   **Flagship:** `Water Risk & Permit Readiness Packet`
*   **Others:** `Water Cost Exposure Statement`, `Permitting Readiness Memo`.

### 2.3 Monitoring Artifacts (Living)
Dynamic outputs tracking changes over time. Not reliance-grade unless "frozen".
*   `Portfolio Water Risk Register`
*   `Rate Change Monitor`
*   `Evidence Integrity Alerts`

### 2.4 Advisory Artifacts (Decision Support)
AI-assisted synthesis to accelerate understanding. clearly labeled as "Advisory".
*   `Executive Briefing Synthesis`
*   `Scenario Narratives`

---

## 3. Flagship: Water Risk & Permit Readiness Packet
A Frozen Decision Artifact designed to attach directly to:
*   Underwriting files & Diligence packages
*   Investment Committee materials

**Standard Sections:**
1.  **Scope**: Asset/Location boundaries.
2.  **Rate Intelligence**: Verified Water/Wastewater schedules.
3.  **Permit Authority**: Who grants what, gating risks.
4.  **Availability**: Aquifer/Watershed stress indicators.
5.  **Evidence Log**: Source list, retrieval dates.

**Explicit Non-Claims:**
*   It is NOT engineering certification.
*   It is NOT legal counsel.

## 4. Workflow Mapping

### 🏦 Underwriting (Lenders/Insurers)
| Stage | Question | Artifact | Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Intake** | Is water a gating risk? | `Site Water Feasibility Brief` | Frozen |
| **Pricing** | Verification of cost? | `Water Cost Exposure Statement` | Frozen |
| **Close** | Basis of reliance? | `Readiness Packet` | Frozen |
| **Portfolio** | What changed? | `Change Monitor` | Monitoring |

### 🏢 Site Selection (Data Centers/Industrial)
| Stage | Question | Artifact | Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Screen** | Which sites fail? | `Feasibility Brief` | Frozen |
| **Shortlist** | Comparative risk? | `Comparative Matrix` | Frozen |
| **Final** | Decision record? | `Readiness Packet` | Frozen |

## 5. Commercial Packaging
1.  **Transaction Deliverables (Paid)**: The "Frozen" packets (Risk, Cost, Permitting).
2.  **Subscription (Recurring)**: Portfolio monitoring, Change alerts, Re-freeze triggers.
3.  **Enterprise Integration**: Workflow embedding for high-volume partners.

---

## 6. Risks & Controls
*   **Risk:** Buyers treat advisory outputs as authoritative.
    *   *Control:* Strict labeling and reliance posture discipline.
*   **Risk:** Staleness causes reliance failure.
    *   *Control:* Monitoring outputs trigger "Re-freeze" events.
*   **Risk:** Coverage gaps create blind spots.
    *   *Control:* Explicit "Unknown" states in Frozen artifacts.
