## 2. The Problem: Water Risk IS Financial Risk
In the US Sun Belt (starting with Florida), water availability is no longer a given—it is a **material financial constraint**.

*   **For Lenders:** Loans are underwritten against assets that may lack long-term utility capacity.
*   **For Insurers:** Actuarial models miss "Ghost Inventory" (expired capacity) and hidden regulatory liens.
*   **For Site Selectors:** Billion-dollar industrial sites are selected on "Letters of Availability" that are often legally worthless.

Decision-makers are forcing capital into a market defined by **opacity, fragmentation, and regulatory complexity**.
